Hypnotoad

Futurama once featured Hypnotoad in a 22-minute episode entitled "Everybody Loves Hypnotoad." It featured Hypnotoad's trademark droning, as well occassional establishing shots and sound effects.

  • Scientific Name: Bufo Groening
  • Average Length: 50 centimeters
  • Average Rank: First Place
  • Call: Angry Machine
  • Related Species: V-Frog

Hypnotoad has become a celebrity independent of the relatively few cameo appearances on the show Futurama. All Glory to the Hypnotoad!
